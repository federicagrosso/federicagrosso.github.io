👋 Hi, I am Federica Grosso!

I am a PhD candidate at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam and a research fellow at IRGB-CNR. My work focuses on Mendelian Randomization, genome-wide association studies, and causal inference in longitudinal data.

Previously, I completed my MSc in Biostatistics at University of Milano-Bicocca and BSc in Mathematics at University of Milan.
