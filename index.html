Federica Grosso

PhD Candidate @CNCR-CTGlab · Statistical Genetics · VU Amsterdam

👋 Hello world!

My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a Master's degree in Biostatistics. During my Master's thesis at IRGB-CNR in Cagliari, I developed a strong interest in genetics, which led me to work as a researcher using techniques such as Mendelian Randomization and exploring causal inference for longitudinal data. I am now a PhD candidate at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, focusing on the development of methods to uncover new insights into complex traits, particularly neuropsychiatric disorders.

Previously, I worked on microbiome and age-related traits, using Two-Sample Mendelian Randomization and other causal inference methods.
