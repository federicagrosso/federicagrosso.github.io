Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, focusing on the development of methods to uncover new insights into complex traits, particularly neuropsychiatric disorders.

My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a Master's degree in Biostatistics.

During my Master's thesis at IRGB-CNR in Cagliari, I developed a strong interest in genetics. I later worked as a researcher using Mendelian Randomization and exploring causal inference for longitudinal data.

Previously, I worked on microbiome and age-related traits using Two-Sample Mendelian Randomization and related methods.
